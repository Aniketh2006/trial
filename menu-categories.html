Our Philosophy

Sarigama Bharathi Music School, Parkala Udupi, has been a beacon of cultural learning for the past 25 years. This institution has not only provided high-quality music education, but also created hundreds of music enthusiasts across Udupi and beyond.

More than just teaching, the school has offered students performance opportunities, honored local talents, and built a nurturing space for the growth of Indian art forms.

Alongside music, we offer Yakshagana, Classical dance, Harikatha, bhajans, and Vedic studies — bringing students close to the cultural richness of Indian heritage.

The institution continues to grow with the blessings of the community, inspiring new generations of artists.

What we offer

  • Classical vocal training (Carnatic)
  • Performance workshops & recitals
  • Customized learning plans for children and adults
